# Schema Showcase Feature - Added to Roadmap

## Summary

Successfully added the **Schema Showcase** feature to the Objectified Feature Roadmap. This feature will recognize and promote companies with top-quality schemas on browse.objectified.dev.

---

## 🏆 Schema Showcase Feature Overview

### Purpose
Create a monthly showcase on browse.objectified.dev that highlights schemas with the highest quality scores (90-100), providing recognition to companies that demonstrate API design excellence.

### Key Features Added

#### 1. Showcase Gallery (browse.objectified.dev)
- **Monthly rotation** of featured schemas
- **Hero section** for "Schema of the Month"
- **Category-based organization**:
    - Industry Leaders (Financial, Healthcare, E-commerce, SaaS)
    - Best Practices (Documentation, Composition, Architecture)
    - Innovation Awards (Novel patterns, Complex modeling)
    - Community Choice (Most cloned/forked, Highest ratings)

#### 2. Eligibility Requirements
- **Quality Score**: Minimum 90/100, maintained for 30+ days
- **No Critical Violations**: Clean schema validation
- **Documentation**: Complete descriptions for classes and properties
- **Active Maintenance**: Updated within last 90 days
- **Public or Opt-in**: Schema must be accessible

#### 3. Company Benefits
- **Homepage Feature**: Prominent placement on browse.objectified.dev
- **Social Media Promotion**: Twitter, LinkedIn posts by Objectified
- **Marketing Assets**:
    - "Featured Schema" logo badge
    - Digital certificates
    - Social sharing cards
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Views, clones, traffic referrals
- **Press Opportunities**: Case studies, testimonials

#### 4. Selection Process
- **Automatic Detection**: System scans weekly for eligible schemas
- **Manual Nomination**: Companies and community can nominate
- **Editorial Review**: Quality, diversity, educational value
- **Opt-out Available**: Privacy-focused option

#### 5. Showcase Features
- **Schema Cards**: Logo, title, quality score, preview thumbnail
- **Detail Pages**: Full visualization, metrics, company spotlight
- **Archive**: Browse past showcases, search by category/date
- **Hall of Fame**: Multi-time featured schemas

#### 6. Gamification
- **Achievement Badges**:
    - Featured Schema
    - Schema of the Month
    - Category Leader
    - Hall of Fame (3+ features)
- **Leaderboards**: Top schemas by quality, most featured companies
- **Rewards**: Extended trials, premium features, beta access

#### 7. Integration
- **Quality Score Monitoring**: Automatic removal if score drops below 85
- **Template Marketplace**: Featured schemas become templates
- **Social Media**: Auto-generated cards, scheduled posts
- **Email Campaigns**: Monthly showcase newsletter
