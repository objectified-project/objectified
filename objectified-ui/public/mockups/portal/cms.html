# Webhooks 101

Webhooks are how we **push** events to your systems
in near real time. This guide covers signing,
verification, retries, and replay.

## Signing

Every delivery includes a header:

<CodeBlock language="bash">
X-Acme-Signature: t=1705428000,v1=...
</CodeBlock>

Use <EndpointCard endpoint="POST /webhooks/replay" />
to retry deliveries from the dashboard.
