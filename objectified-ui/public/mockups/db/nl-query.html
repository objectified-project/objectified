Your question

Show me enterprise customers in Germany whose Q2 spend dropped more than 30% versus Q1, including their top 3 missing SKUs.

Schema · prod-2026-04 Confidence 92 % 3 412 ms · 1 487 tokens

Plan & reasoning

  1. 1

    Resolve entities

    Mapped "enterprise customers" → Customer.tier = "ent", "Germany" → Address.country = "DE".

  2. 2

    Aggregate per quarter

    Two CTEs: Q1 (Jan–Mar) and Q2 (Apr–Jun) sums of Order.total_cents grouped by customer.

  3. 3

    Filter declines

    Keep rows where (q2 − q1) / NULLIF(q1, 0) ≤ −0.30.

  4. 4

    Lateral join · top SKUs

    For each customer, lateral join to historical wishlist / catalog vectors and surface 3 SKUs not present in any Q2 order.
