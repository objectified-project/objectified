What changes vs today. The header above replaces today's center rail (Home · Control Panel · Designer · Paths). Every app in the suite is now reachable in one click via the app pill (2) or in zero clicks via ⌘ K (4). The middle of the header now answers the most-asked question — "what project and branch am I editing?" — instead of repeating the app's own name. Each app gets its own sub-nav (8), so switching apps doesn't shrink your work area.
