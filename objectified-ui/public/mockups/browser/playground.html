Paste an OpenAPI, Arazzo, or JSON Schema document, or load one from a URL. Nothing is uploaded — everything runs in your browser. Validation, outline, and diff work just like the published viewer.

Your spec stays in this browser tab.

Pasted/uploaded content is parsed locally with js-yaml and validated with @stoplight/spectral-core. Sharing encodes the spec into the URL fragment (or session storage if > 32 KB).
