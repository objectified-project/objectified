Enterprise Hub mockups

Visual designs for the Objectified Enterprise Hub — a centralized console for platform teams managing Objectified at organizational scale. Aggregated tenant operations, cost governance with chargeback, organization-wide policy enforcement, and a self-service service catalog. Mockups follow the Studio design system: Inter typography, indigo accent, slate gray scale, panel-based layout, and dark mode parity.
