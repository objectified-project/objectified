SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

Effective 2025-11-04 · Expires 2026-11-03 · Auto-renew enabled

This Service Level Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Acme Corp ("Provider") and Globex ("Consumer"), each a "Party" and together "Parties".

§ 1. Uptime Guarantee

Provider warrants 99.95% monthly uptime, excluding scheduled maintenance windows announced ≥ 72 hours in advance, capped at 4 hours per month.

§ 2. Response Latency

p95 response latency for GET endpoints shall not exceed 200 ms, measured over a rolling 24-hour window.

§ 3. Rate Limits

Consumer may issue up to 2 000 requests per minute. Bursts up to 2× are tolerated for < 60 seconds.

§ 4. Breach Credits

Failure to meet uptime SLA results in service credits scaled to the magnitude of the breach (5%, 15%, 25%) applied to the next monthly invoice.
