About this course

Composition is the secret weapon of well-designed APIs. By the end of this course you will be comfortable modeling polymorphic types, expressing constraints declaratively, and avoiding the most common refactor traps. You'll ship a complete payments-style schema as your capstone, scored against a real-world rubric.

What you'll learn

  • When to reach for allOf vs oneOf
  • Designing discriminated unions
  • Multi-tenant isolation patterns
  • Schema versioning trade-offs
  • Inline vs $ref refactor calculus
  • Validation across the request lifecycle
